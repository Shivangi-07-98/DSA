0 is actually an even number.
Because an even number means divisible by 2 with remainder 0
And: 0 % 2 = 0
So 0 is even.

Examples of even numbers:
... -4, -2, 0, 2, 4, 6 ...

Examples of odd numbers:
... -3, -1, 1, 3, 5 ...
